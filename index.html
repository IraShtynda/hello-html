HOW TO MAKE A GREAT SMOOTHIE?

Some tips for making a healthy smoothie:

  • Choose a liquid base
  • Add vegetables for a dose of greens.
  • Consider adding healthy fats.
  • Superfood & protein add-ins are a plus.
